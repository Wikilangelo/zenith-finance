# Motion & Animation System

## Philosophy

Animations must feel:

- calm
- organic
- intelligent

## Motion Style

Use:

- spring animations
- opacity fades
- subtle scale transitions
- soft skeleton loading

Avoid:

- aggressive bounce
- excessive motion
- distracting animations

## Timing

Fast:

- 150ms

Normal:

- 250ms

Complex:

- 400ms

## AI Thinking States

AI loading states should:

- reassure users
- reduce anxiety
- communicate progress

Use:

- shimmering skeletons
- subtle glow
- progressive reveal

## Page Transitions

Pages should:

- fade
- slightly slide
- preserve continuity
